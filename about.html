About

Designer & Developer

I am a Louisville-based designer/developer/strategist originally from Southern California. I graduated summa cum laude with my BFA in graphic design from University of Louisville in 2020. Since graduating I have worked on over 50 products, websites, and brands as designer, developer, strategist, or some blend of the three. I currently work in Humana's experience design and strategy organization as a product designer.



I most enjoy finding innovative and compelling ways to tell stories and make an impact in digital spaces. Many of my favorite projects have been with non-profits and organizations that seek to leave the world a better place than we found it.
