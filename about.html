About

Designer, developer, hiker, plant parent, & sci-fi nerd

I’m a Louisville-based designer/developer with 5 years of experience creating human-centered products and websites, currently working as a product designer at Humana. I graduated summa cum laude with my BFA in graphic design from University of Louisville in 2020. Since graduating, I have worked on over 50 products, websites, and brands as designer, developer, experience strategist, or some blend of the three. Although my experience spans local agencies, large corporations, startups, and non-profits alike, my favorite projects have been those that aim to leave the world a better place than we found it.



Outside of work, I try to apply the same passion I have for designing to the things I do in my free time, whether that’s hiking, painting, or taking care of [probably too many] plants. I also spend a lot of time learning; I find I get the most out of life if I approach it with curiosity.
